Activities


    Start off your day with surf lessons in the morning. At lunch, chill at the pool enjoying local delicacies and hang out, read books or just tan! In the afternoon, we will offer coding classes for different levels (Python, HTML, CSS...). In the evening, we offer yoga classes, beach parties and movie nights. We will do our best to make your stay as memorable as possible!

Sunset yoga

After making use of your physical strength at surfing and of your mental strength at coding, finish off the day with a session of sunset yoga on the beach.

Discover Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka is full of culture, colors and surprises. Take a day off and go explore the beautiful surroundings!

Personal mentor

Are you planning on starting your own website/app? We are here to help you! Our experienced trainees will help you get closer to your dream.
